Who We Are

Wesalvatore is a dedicated team of animal lovers, rescuers, and advocates committed to saving and improving the lives of animals in need. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals, connect them with loving homes, and collaborate with NGOs to create a better world for all creatures.

What We Do

Animal Rescue

We rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide medical care, and ensure they are safe and healthy.

Adoption

We match rescued animals with loving families, ensuring they find forever homes.

NGO Collaboration

We partner with NGOs to amplify our impact and create a network of support for animals in need.
